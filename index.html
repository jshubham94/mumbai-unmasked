Welcome

Mumbai is a city of dreams — but the reality for its 20 million residents often tells a different story. This platform aims to highlight systemic issues in governance and public infrastructure managed by the BMC (Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation).

With verified data, news reports, and on-ground evidence, we aim to bring awareness, spark conversation, and encourage civic engagement.
