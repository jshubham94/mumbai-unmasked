Welcome

Mumbai is a city of dreams — but the reality for its 20 million residents often tells a different story. This platform highlights systemic issues in governance and infrastructure, especially those handled by the BMC (Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation).

We back our claims with verified data, on-ground stories, and credible reporting to raise awareness and drive accountability.

Issues

  • Poor Road Quality: Over 27,000 potholes were filled in 2023 alone — yet the roads remain treacherous.
  • Garbage Management: Overflowing bins, unsegregated waste, and illegal dumping persist in several wards.
  • Flooding: Areas like Hindmata, Sion, and Chembur face waterlogging every monsoon due to drainage failure.
  • Corruption & Contracts: Inflated project costs and irregularities plague civic tenders.
  • Public Health: Shortage of functioning toilets, hospital bed scams, and outbreaks due to sanitation lapses.

Case Studies

  • Dadar Floods (2023): In just 40 minutes, low-lying areas were submerged. Drainage systems were non-functional.
  • Deonar Fire (2016): Toxic smoke from the landfill choked citizens for days — repeated fires went unaddressed.
  • Elphinstone Stampede (2017): 23 lives lost due to years of delay in widening a narrow footbridge.
  • RTI Exposé (2022): ₹6,000+ crore spent on roads, yet no clear audit trail was made available publicly.
